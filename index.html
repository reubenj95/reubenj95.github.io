About the Author

Kia ora e te whānau,

I’m Reuben, and I’m based in Pōneke – more specifically in the tropical oasis of Taitā in Lower Hutt where I live with my wife Zoe, and our fur babies Andy (cat) and Bellamy (dog).

The Identity and values section, probably combined with the echo chamber of being home alone this week, has definitely got me thinking hard about this values stuff. I think the biggest theme for me at the moment is learning about yourself (myself?). I’ve always been a bit of a philomath, hoarding new knowledge like some kind of information dragon. Until maybe a year ago, I thought that I had a really good handle on my self-concept but a few big life experiences and fair amount of time and money spent on a top-notch therapist has been questioning a lot of those things. So those learning muscles have been turned inwards and I’m finding that I maybe had some assumptions abouut who/what/when/where I am that don’t hold so true anymore.

It’s certainly not easy to re-learn who you are and what you’re about, but I absolutely think it’s a good thing. After all, it’s a pretty big part of why I’m here! I studied and have subsequently worked in marketing for my whole career (aside from a brief, and terrible stint in sales *shudders*) and I always thought that was my ‘thing’. Turns out, what I liked about my early career, like the techy stuff, setting up automations and doing campaign reporting and all that jazz, was what I found fun and it all disappears as you get better at marketing and move into management type roles. Go figure!

All that to say, I’m stoked to be here and to embrace my nerdy, techy, build things that do cool stuff side with you all!
